Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) is a crop insurance scheme launched by the Government of India in 2016. It aims to provide financial protection to farmers against crop losses due to natural calamities, pests, and diseases, and ensure their financial stability.

The main objective of PMFBY is to provide affordable crop insurance coverage to farmers and safeguard their livelihood by compensating for crop losses and ensuring their financial stability.
